About CPCCU

Who We Are

Competitive Programming Camp City University (CPCCU) is a student-led programming community dedicated to improving problem-solving skills, algorithmic thinking, and programming excellence.

Mission

To inspire students to explore competitive programming, participate in contests, and become skilled software developers.

Vision

To establish City University as a leading institution for programming excellence and ICPC participation.
