About CPCCU

Empowering students through competitive programming, problem solving, and technical excellence.

Who We Are

Competitive Programming Camp City University (CPCCU) is a student-led programming community dedicated to helping students develop strong problem-solving abilities through competitive programming, algorithmic thinking, and continuous learning.


We organize programming contests, workshops, bootcamps, and collaborative learning sessions to create an environment where students can improve their technical skills while building a strong programming culture at City University.

🎯 Mission

To inspire students to embrace competitive programming and develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

🚀 Vision

To establish City University as a recognized hub for programming excellence and ICPC participation.

💡 Activities

Programming contests, workshops, seminars, ICPC preparation camps, and community learning initiatives.

Our Journey

2024

Formation of CPCCU and launch of initial programming activities.

2025

Organized workshops, contests, and competitive programming training sessions.

2026

Expanded community engagement and strengthened ICPC preparation initiatives.
